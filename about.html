What is CodeUp?

Set up by Sunil Bishnoi in early 2019, Tutor Up is the Personnol Assitant to Student as Tutor, Guide. Initially running out of borrowed office space in central Jodhpur , the aim was to provide best and friendly Math and Physics, English tuition to anyone who wanted it. Since its just started i wanted to help student and become strong at subject and get ahead in their Career.

Where do I go?

We currently run the event in the offices of Code Computerlove, a fantastic digital agency set right in the heart of the Northern Quarter. The event runs after normal working hours so we have to use the trade entrance, but directions and entry instructions can be found here.

What happens?

We usually start the session with weak subject of student and prepare him/her with better example and build strong concept.

We encourage student to share and tell in which subject or topic they feel weak on which I will try to help them to build strong concepts.
